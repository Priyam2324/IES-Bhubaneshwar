About IEEE IES Bhubaneswar Chapter

The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Bhubaneswar Chapter is a vibrant professional and academic hub under the IEEE Bhubaneswar Section. It is committed to promoting innovation, research, and education in industrial electronics, automation, robotics, smart systems, and Industry 4.0 technologies.

The chapter acts as a collaborative platform for academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students across Bhubaneswar and Odisha, facilitating technical discussions, knowledge sharing, and real-world project development.

Vision

To build a dynamic community of professionals and students dedicated to driving innovation and excellence in industrial electronics and related technologies.

Objectives

  • Advance research in industrial electronics, control systems, power systems, AI, and IoT.
  • Enable smooth technology transfer between academia and industry.
  • Build a skilled workforce for Industry 4.0 and 5.0 challenges.
  • Foster collaboration with IEEE IES communities across India and worldwide.
  • Organize conferences, workshops, seminars, and talks regularly.
  • Support student chapters with mentoring, internships, and publishing help.
  • Encourage innovation projects and IEEE-aligned research publications.
  • Host hackathons, conclaves, and themed events like IEEE Day and WiE programs.

Student Engagement

The chapter actively collaborates with student branches at:

  • KIIT Deemed to be University
  • IIT Bhubaneswar
  • Silicon Institute of Technology
  • SOA University
  • College of Engineering and Technology (CET)

Student branches regularly organize:

  • Weekly hands-on workshops (Arduino, robotics, MATLAB, etc.)
  • Paper writing & publication mentorship sessions
  • Mini hackathons and technical exhibitions
  • Guided internships and IEEE research mentoring

Benefits for IEEE IES Student Members:

  • Involvement in funded projects and IEEE global initiatives
  • Opportunities to present at international IEEE IES conferences
  • Access to IEEE scholarships, travel grants, and awards

Collaboration & Industry Engagement

  • Strategic partnerships with local industries and MSMEs
  • Collaborations with DRDO, ISRO, and CDAC for technology exchange
  • Support for internships and placements with electronics & power companies
  • Participation in IEEE global standards and innovation programs
