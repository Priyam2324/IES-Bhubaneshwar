About IEEE IES Bhubaneswar Chapter

The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Bhubaneswar Chapter is a dynamic professional and academic hub under the IEEE Bhubaneswar Section. It is committed to fostering technological advancement and interdisciplinary collaboration in areas such as industrial electronics, automation, robotics, control systems, smart technologies, and Industry 4.0 applications. As a collaborative platform, the chapter connects academicians, researchers, industry professionals, entrepreneurs, and students from Bhubaneswar and across Odisha. Through diverse activities—including technical seminars, workshops, lectures, panel discussions, hackathons, and industrial visits—it facilitates knowledge sharing, innovation, and impactful real-world project development. Beyond technical growth, the chapter supports professional development and leadership among young engineers and scholars. It offers opportunities for mentorship, publishing, and interdisciplinary research addressing current engineering challenges. By promoting curiosity, collaboration, and lifelong learning, the IEEE IES Bhubaneswar Chapter continues to shape the future of industrial electronics and smart systems in the region and beyond.
