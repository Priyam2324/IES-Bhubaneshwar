About IEEE IES Bhubaneswar Section

The IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES) Bhubaneswar Section is a dynamic professional body operating under the IEEE Bhubaneswar umbrella, dedicated to the advancement of research, innovation, and education in industrial electronics across Eastern India. Established to promote collaboration among academia, industry professionals, and researchers, the Section focuses on pioneering developments in areas such as automation, control systems, power electronics, embedded systems, and industrial informatics. It organizes technical talks, workshops, seminars, student-driven initiatives, and academic-industry engagements to uphold IEEE's mission of advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. By actively involving students, young professionals, and experienced experts, the Section serves as a hub for knowledge sharing and skill development. Through its emphasis on innovation and interdisciplinary research, the IEEE IES Bhubaneswar Section plays a vital role in strengthening the regional landscape of industrial electronics and its allied fields.
